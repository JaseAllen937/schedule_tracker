    📖 How to Use Momentum Tracker

    🏗️ Step 1: Build Your Daily System

    1. Go to "Build Habits"

    2. Click "Add New Category" (e.g., School, Fitness, Health)

    3. Click "+ Add Task" to add specific daily tasks

    Example: "30 min: Study French"

    ✅ Step 2: Complete Your Day

    1. Check off each task as you complete it (✓)

    2. When ALL tasks are done, click the green "COMPLETE TODAY" button

    3. Your streak increases by 1 day!

    🚫 Step 3: Track Bad Habits (Optional)

    1. Go to "Break Habits"

    2. Click "+ Track New Habit"

    3. Enter what you're quitting (e.g., "Pornography", "Smoking")

    4. Click "Mark Relapse" if you slip up - it resets the counter

    🎯 Step 4: Set Goals (Optional)

    • Add 30-day milestones with target dates

    • Set your ultimate end goal (e.g., "$1,000/month by March")

    ⚠️ Important Rules:

    • You can only complete a day ONCE per 24 hours

    • If you miss a day, your streak resets to 0

    • You must check ALL tasks before completing the day

    • Task checkboxes reset every day after completion

    💡 Pro Tips:

    • Start with 2-3 tasks max - don't overwhelm yourself

    • Make tasks specific and time-bound

    • Complete your hardest task first thing in the morning

    • Your longest streak proves what you're capable of
